About

Artist development.
Music rights management.

Anemoia is a New York City-based artist development and music rights management company.

Launched in 2023 by respected Atlantic Records A&R Executive Ian Swift Hunter, Anemoia specializes in the early development of artists whose innovative music and singular visions place them at the forefront of their emerging scenes.

Through unconventional deal structures and bespoke support services, Anemoia helps build and scale multiple revenue streams unique to each artist brand.
